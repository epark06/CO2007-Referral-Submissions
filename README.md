# Referral Form – README

The referral form is used to collect and submit referral information in a structured and consistent way. This helps ensure that all required details are captured accurately.

## Information Collected

The form collects:

- **Referring Physician Information**
  - Physician’s full name
  - Clinic name

- **Contact Information** (optional)
  - Phone number
  - Email address
  - Fax number

- **Patient Details**
  - Patient’s full name
  - Date of birth

- **Clinical Information**
  - Urgency level
  - Reason for referral

---

## How to Use the Referral Form

### 1. Open the Referral Form
Access the form through the provided system or link.

### 2. Enter Referring Physician Information
- Provide the referring physician’s full name.
- Enter the clinic name associated with the referring physician.

### 3. Enter Patient Details
- Provide the patient’s full name and date of birth.

**Note:**
- Both fields are required.
- The full name must include first and last name, exactly as it appears in official records.

### 4. Provide Clinical Information
- Select the urgency level (Routine, Urgent, or Immediate).
- Enter the reason for referral.

**Note:**
- The reason for referral is limited to 500 characters.

### 5. Review and Submit
Verify all information for accuracy before submitting the form.

---
